About the App

Sudan Board Quiz is a comprehensive mobile application designed specifically to help medical students and professionals prepare for the Sudan Medical Council Local Examination. The app features thousands of carefully curated multiple-choice questions (MCQs) covering all essential topics in medicine, surgery, obstetrics, gynecology, pediatrics, and community medicine.

Our application provides realistic exam simulation, detailed explanations for each question, performance tracking, and personalized study recommendations. Whether you're a recent graduate or an experienced medical professional preparing for certification, SDQ offers the tools you need to succeed on this important examination.
